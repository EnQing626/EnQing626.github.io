About me

I received my M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees under the supervision of Prof. Lijuan Duan in computer science and technology from Beijing University of Technology, Beijing, in 2017 and 2021, respectively. From 2017 to 2021, I interned at the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of Prof. Zhaoxiang Zhang. From October 2019 to April 2020, I interned at MMLab, Chinese University of Hong Kong, as a Research Assistant under the supervision of Prof. Wanli Ouyang.
Currently, I am working as a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Lab at Carleton University under the supervision of Prof. Yuhong Guo.

Research Interests

My research interests include

  • Computer Vision

  • Exemplar Learning

  • Medical Image Segmentation

  • Video Object Segmentation

  • Weakly Supervised Learning
