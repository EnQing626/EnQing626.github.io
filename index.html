About me

I received the M.Sc. and PhD degrees under the supervision of Duan in computer science and technology from Beijing University of Technology, Beijing, in 2017 and 2021, respectively. From 2017 to 2021, I interned at the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences under the supervision of Prof.Zhaoxiang Zhang. From October 2019 to April 2020, I interned at MMLab, Chinese University of Hong Kong as an Research Assistant under the supervision of Prof.Wanli Ouyang. Currently, I work as a Postdoctoral Fellow at Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Lab, Carleton University under the supervision of Prof.Yuhong Guo. My research interests include computer vision, video object segmentation, medical iamge segmentation and weakly supervised learning.
